Research Vision

Core Mission: Developing Multimodal Foundation Models for Robotics that enable autonomous systems to understand and interact with the physical world.

My research bridges abstract AI reasoning with embodied robotics through sensor-driven machine learning, cross-cultural language model analysis, and control theory. I demonstrated that reliability techniques effective in high-resource settings (English/Western contexts) often fail in low-resource environments (Pidgin/Nigerian contexts), using semantic entropy as a measurement framework.
